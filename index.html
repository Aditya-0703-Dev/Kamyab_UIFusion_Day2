About Hindu College of Engineering

The Hindu College of Engineering (HCE), established in 1999 in Sonepat, Haryana, has become a prominent institution for technological education, affiliated with Deenbandhu Chhotu Ram University of Science and Technology (DCRUST) and approved by AICTE. The college offers B.Tech. and M.Tech. programs in key engineering disciplines such as Computer Science, Electronics and Communication, Electrical, Mechanical, and Civil Engineering. HCE has advanced infrastructure with modern laboratories, workshops, and conference halls. Its library is extensive, with access to thousands of books and various international e-journals, enabling students to access a wealth of knowledge in their respective fields.

Our Mission

At Hindu College of Engineering, our mission is to foster an entrepreneurial mindset among students, encouraging innovation, creativity, and collaboration. We strive to provide resources, guidance, and opportunities that empower students to take their ideas to the next level and create a positive impact on society. We believe in cultivating future leaders who are driven by purpose, passion, and the desire to bring about change.
