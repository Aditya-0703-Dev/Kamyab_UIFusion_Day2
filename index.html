About Hindu College of Engineering

The Hindu College of Engineering is embarking on an exciting new era of growth and development. It has come up as an icon of technological learning with a motive to foster a spirit of free and objective enquiry among the students. It instils a sense of purpose to education and helps young learners develop a balanced and integrated personality. It stimulates both heart and mind of technocrats and empowers them to contribute to the welfare of society. The Hindu College of Engineering was established in 1999. The Institute is duly approved by AICTE and is affiliated to Deenbandhu Chhotu Ram University of Science & Technology, Murthal, Sonepat. (http://www.dcrustm.ac.in/)

Our Mission

At Hindu College of Engineering, our mission is to foster an entrepreneurial mindset among students, encouraging innovation, creativity, and collaboration. We strive to provide resources, guidance, and opportunities that empower students to take their ideas to the next level and create a positive impact on society. We believe in cultivating future leaders who are driven by purpose, passion, and the desire to bring about change.
